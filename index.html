About Me


Welcome to my webpage. Here you will find some information about me including my interests both in and out of my career, as well as my education and my work experience over the years.


I am a machine learning engineer early in my career. I recently began my career as a software developer for Leidos. I am a member of the Atlassian tools team, providing support for multiple development teams on our contract using the Atlassian products. I have also had a few internships with a wide range of repsonsibility from testing, to helping write scripts and tools, to web development. I have learned a great deal from all my experiences hope to use all the knowledge I gain to help further my career.

Outside of work, I also enjoy using what I've learned to aid in personal projects. One project that I am currently working on is a surf forecasting dashboard written in Python. It is built using the Plotly Dash framework scraping various websites to find current and forecasted weather conditions to help predict surf. The dashboard offers and easily digestable format for viewing the conditions for multiple locations easily and efficently. Another project I'm working on is a project that analyzes professional surfers wave scores and counts, to try to learn more about what could give surfers an edge in contests. I also enjoy working on smaller projects such as writing a genetic algorithm to solve any size, regular sudoku board.

Aside from programming I enjoy staying physically active. I enjoy surfing, skiing, biking, touch rugby, paddle boarding, playing volleyball, and getting outside.



Education


I hold a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Maryland, with a B.S. in Computer Science. Here I took a few programming courses, focused in Java and object oriented programming, and discrete mathematics.

After earning my Associate Degree from Montgomery College, I transfered to the University of Maryland, continuing my studies in computer science. While at Maryland, I took many courses including Data Structures, Intro to Data Science, Machine Learning, Networks and Security, and Algorithms. Along with my computer science classes, I also took many classes with the Economics department as part of my upper level concentration. While in school, my favorite classes were Intro to Data Science and Machine Learning.

Because of my love for the field I have decided to continue my education by pursuing a Masters Degree in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Drexel University. Here I will have the opportunity to learn a variety of subjects related to the field and apply them to my work place.
